Hello!

Nov 9, 2015

Hi there. Today is a joyous day. It is now, on this - the 10th of November, (and day after the Australian commemoration of 9/11) that we are launching We Press Buttons. We Press Buttons is Dan Hughes, James Carmen, Joshua Robb, Morgan Cave - and last but not least, Matthew Fraser. We're a bunch of friends who have been playing games together and talkin' smack for over ten years. We've decided to bring this quality entertainment to you, the listener. And thus was born a podcast, We Press Play - a weekly discussion of video games and anything tangentially related to video games. This whole venture is a labor of love, from all of us - we hope you enjoy it.
